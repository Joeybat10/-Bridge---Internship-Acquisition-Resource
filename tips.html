Tips and Tricks For Landing That Job

Having a LinkedIn profile that is updated with your latest skills, experience, and a professional photo is massive for "building your brand" and getting your name out there.

This is arguably the most important aspect to focus on when it comes to getting a job/internship. The saying of "its not what you know, its who you know", is a very true statement. Networking can open so many doors that were never though possible.

These are great opportunities to make new connections and get your name out there to maximize your chances of getting an interview.
